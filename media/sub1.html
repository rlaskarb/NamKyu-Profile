ABOUT

STORY

KPop Demon Hunters
Global K-Pop girl group 'Huntrix' are day-time stars and secret demon hunters by night. Their songs create amagical barrier called the 'Honmoon' to keep evil at bay. When a rival K-Pop demon boy band, the 'Saja Boys', threatens to weaken the barrier, Huntrix must face their biggest challenge yet. Meanwhile, leader Rumi hides her secret of having demon blood, which causes her voice to falter. To protect her fans and find redemption, she risks everything in a final showdown at their last concert.

HUNTR/X

  • The Silent Legacy
    A Huntrix, a silent guardian of a sacred duty passed down through generations. Her eyes, though sharp, held the somber wisdom of centuries-old tradition. She was a master of mystic arts, her power a heavy, inherited legacy. She moved with a quiet grace, her story a prologue written in shadows and whispers. A life not yet her own, she danced with cold steel between duty and destiny, waiting for the vibrant symphony she would one day become.
    Legacy Transformed
    Now, Huntrix commands the stage, shedding her heavy robes for a bold, modern persona. Her heritage, once a burden, is now a source of strength as she leads her team as a fearless trendsetter. She has rewritten her own story, creating a vibrant, unstoppable force. With a defiant finger pointed to the sky, she owns her past while fearlessly carving her future. Her stage is her temple, her music her magic, and her future a boundless canvas she paints with every beat.

SAJA BOYS

  • A Sweet & Bubbly Disguise
    Their charm is as effervescent as soda pop. With bright smiles and infectious energy, they dance through a neon-lit city, every move a sweet invitation to a world of carefree joy. This is their light-hearted disguise, a meticulously crafted performance designed to win your heart and lower your guard. Beneath their perfectly choreographed smiles lies a hidden agenda, a sugar-coated promise of what life could be if you just followed their beat.
    The Unraveling of the Soul
    Beneath the playful facade lies a darker truth. The SAJA Boys now emerge as haunting, shadowy figures in a broken world. Their temptation shifts from bright allure to a chilling, mind-bending influence. With unsettling grace, they dance not to create joy, but to sow chaos and confusion. This is a destructive allure, a whisper that promises power in exchange for your very identity. They are the idols who lure you not with happiness, but with the seductive idea of losing yourself completely, a final, irreversible seduction.

DUFFY

  • The Wild Guardian
    Duffy, a true guardian with a heart as wild as his form. He may have the fangs of a demon tiger, but his fierce gaze is a shield for his friends, not a weapon against them. His powerful stance and intimidating roar are born from a boundless loyalty and the simple desire to protect those he cares for. In a chaotic world, he is a force of nature, but one that stands firmly on the side of good, ready to break through any darkness to keep his loved ones safe.
    The Messenger of Hope
    Here, Duffy's true nature is revealed. He is not just a fierce protector, but a loyal and gentle companion. Holding a small note, he becomes a bridge between hearts, a silent messenger of sincerity. His wild eyes soften, reflecting a deep connection and unwavering loyalty to his friend, Jinu. In this moment, the ferocious tiger is gone, replaced by a gentle soul who carries not a weapon, but a fragile message of hope.
