7 Straight Weeks at
#1 on Billboard!

"Golden," the official soundtrack for the Netflix animated film 'K-POP DEMON HUNTERS,' has topped the Billboard Hot 100 for a seventh non-consecutive week. According to Billboard, the track by the film's virtual girl group, Huntrix, secured the #1 spot on the chart dated October 4. Having remained on the chart for 14 consecutive weeks, "Golden" is now the only K-pop song other than BTS's "Butter" (10 weeks) to spend seven or more weeks at the top.

Sajaboyz Hits the
Billboard Top 10

Huntrix's rivals, Sajaboyz, also found success, landing two top-10 hits with "Your Idol" (#4) and "Soda Pop" (#10), a new career-high for the group. The series, K-POP DEMON HUNTERS, follows the idol group Huntrix as they protect the world from evil spirits through music. Praised for its captivating portrayal of Korean culture, the show has become a global phenomenon. It is now Netflix's most-watched content of all time, surpassing mega-hits like Squid Game and Wednesday.
